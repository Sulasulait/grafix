Our Story

The Studio

"Design is not just what it looks like and feels like. Design is how it works."

— Our Studio Philosophy

We're a team of makers obsessed with craft.

PixelCraft was founded in 2016 with a single conviction: that exceptional design is the most powerful tool a business has. We started as two designers working from a small studio in Kampala, and have since grown into a 12-person team working with brands across four continents.

What hasn't changed is our commitment to doing the work properly — no shortcuts, no templates, no "good enough." Every project receives our full creative attention, our most honest strategic thinking, and our relentless eye for detail.

01

Craft Over Speed

We'd rather take the time to get it right than rush work we'd be embarrassed to show.

02

Honest Collaboration

We tell our clients what we actually think. Good relationships are built on candour, not yes.

03

Function + Beauty

Great design must do its job first. Beauty is how we make the job worth doing.
